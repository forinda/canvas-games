# Flappy Bird — Tutorial

Build a complete **Flappy Bird** clone from scratch using TypeScript and HTML5 Canvas.

**Difficulty:** Intermediate
**Episodes:** 5
**Time:** ~1h 15min total
**Prerequisites:** [Breakout](../12-breakout/README.md)

## What You'll Build

A side-scrolling game where you tap or click to flap a bird upward against gravity. Navigate through gaps in scrolling pipes to survive as long as possible.

## Concepts You'll Learn

- Gravity and jump physics (velocity + acceleration)
- Infinite side-scrolling with pipe recycling
- Gap-based obstacle generation
- Sprite rotation based on velocity
- Screen-shake and death effects

## Episodes

| # | Title | Time | What You'll Build |
|---|-------|------|-------------------|
| 1 | [Project Setup & Bird Rendering](./step-1.md) | ~15min | Draw a bird sprite and a scrolling background |

| 3 | [Pipe Scrolling & Gaps](./step-3.md) | ~15min | Generate and scroll pipes with random gaps |
| 4 | [Collision & Scoring](./step-4.md) | ~15min | Detect pipe/ground collisions, score for passing pipes |
| 5 | [Polish & Effects](./step-5.md) | ~15min | Bird tilt, screen shake on death, medal system |
