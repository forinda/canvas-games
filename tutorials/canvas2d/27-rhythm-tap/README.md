# Rhythm Tap — Tutorial

Build a complete **Rhythm Tap** game from scratch using TypeScript and HTML5 Canvas.

**Difficulty:** Intermediate
**Episodes:** 5
**Time:** ~1h 15min total
**Prerequisites:** [Typing Speed](../26-typing-speed/README.md)

## What You'll Build

A rhythm game where notes scroll down lanes toward a hit zone. Press the correct key when a note reaches the target line. Time your taps perfectly for bonus points.

## Concepts You'll Learn

- Beat maps and note timing from predefined charts
- Timing windows (perfect, great, good, miss)
- Multi-lane scrolling note rendering
- Combo multiplier system
- Audio synchronization basics

## Episodes

| # | Title | Time | What You'll Build |
|---|-------|------|-------------------|
| 1 | [Project Setup & Lane Rendering](./step-1.md) | ~15min | Draw scrolling lanes with a hit zone at the bottom |
| 2 | [Notes & Scrolling](./step-2.md) | ~15min | Notes scroll downward at a steady speed |
| 3 | [Tap Input & Timing Windows](./step-3.md) | ~15min | Press keys to hit notes, grade timing accuracy |
| 4 | [Combo & Scoring](./step-4.md) | ~15min | Build combos for consecutive hits, multiplier scoring |
| 5 | [Beat Maps & Polish](./step-5.md) | ~15min | Define songs as beat maps, hit effects, results screen |
